How to Play

Guide the lemmings from the entrance (top-left) to the exit (bottom-right)!

Select a tool and click on a lemming to assign it. Each tool changes lemming behavior.

Tools: Blocker (stops others) | Digger (digs down) | Builder (builds stairs) | Basher (digs sideways) | Umbrella (survives falls)
